—About Us—

At DARE Business Systems, we believe businesses deserve a single firm, who can service both Information Systems and Bookkeeping requirements, in a straight forward manner, from one place.
DARE Business Systems are also contributing to social well being via regular contribution in Australia, in the area of healthcare and disability, as part of our regular business practices.
Providing more time for business owners to concentrate on running their business, and a deeper relationship and understanding with your service provider.
If you are a business who wants to support the wider community and could benefit from a provider skilled in Information Systems, Bookkeeping and Accounting, give us a call to discuss our potential relationship.

—Meet the Team—

Kylie Dare

Managing Director

Kylie Dare, Founder of DARE Business Systems has decades of experience supporting business operations, software and hardware, and managing staff and projects, and bookkeeping.

From Client Relationship Management systems, Financial Accounting Software and Operations Software, to installing EFTPOS machines for banks, printers, and organising programming between systems.

Kylie has experience supporting over 400 businesses, in diverse environments, such as: Franchise, Corporate Head Office and Branches, in Service, Retail, Wholesale, Importing and Manufacturing and Disability Services industries.

Kylie completed a Diploma of Accounting at Chisholm TAFE, and went on to study Accounting at Monash University, before switching to Business Information Systems at Swinburne University.

Kylie has also completed a number of short courses, a sample of which includes: SQL Querying Databases, core subjects for the Microsoft Systems Engineer, Business Activity Statement requirements to lodge BAS returns, and the H+R Block Tax Agent Course. Combining Accounting and Information Systems skills.

Kylie has a variety of software experience with: Trade Gecko, Sybiz, QuickBooks, MYOB, XERO, Ostendo, Marlin, JavaScript, FTP, plus many more.

Kylie has worked for software companies such as IBM, MYOB, Megabus, and Accounting and Tax firms.

Kylie has also whilst in Accounting Firms, assisted with Company registration requirements, tax returns, bookkeeping and rebuilding accounts.

Mentored by Globally by Experienced Harvard Business School, graduate and Baker Scholar, Bill Lang, and William Buck's General Manager Fi Slavern, Kylie is well supported in business.

Kylie is interested in social well being and giving back to the wider community as part of DARE Business Systems regular practices through regular donations to Soldier On and ASPECT Australia.
